# Data Analyst
## Statistics
A key part of data analysts role is statistics. Statistics can be split into:
- descriptive - summariese the data
- inferential - use a sample to predict
remember stats can't explain why!

Measures of center
- mean
- medium (works for non-symetrical data too)
- mode

Measures of spread
- range 
- variance
  - stadard deviation
- quartiles

Measures of chances
- independant
- condiitonal

Probability distribution
- uniform distribution
  - 
- normal distribution
  - symmetrical
  - kurtoisos
  - skewness (right or left skewed)
  - central limit therom
- binomial distribution
  - n,p
  - independant
- poisson distribution
  - time aspect
  - lamda- average no. of events in a t period

Hypothesis testing
- Ho - assume nothing has changed
- H1 - alternatives exists - difference exists or the direction

1. define population
2. define Ho H1
3. collect sample
4. statistical tests
5. draw conclusion

experiments
- what is the effect of a treament?
- treatment = independant and response is dependant
- control group
  - random selection
  - random blind group
  - random double blind group - both the treater and patient doesn't know

intrepreting results
- correlation - pearson (0-1) 
- significance level - threshold to reject Ho
- p < lamda - reject Ho
- error types 
  - type 1 - Ho is true and Ho is rejected incorrectly (false positive)
  - type 2 - Ho is false and Ho is accepted incorrectly (false negative)
