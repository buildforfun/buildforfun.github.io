## Being a Gentleman
After watching the 2024 Netflix TV show called The Gentlemen, it reminded me of how a true gentleman is able to operate elegantly in life. 
I mean not everyone is a Duke that get's thrown into a world of drugs, but we can learn lessons from the Duke.

### Traits and how to be a Gentleman
- Respectful and be kind to others
  - Think before you speak. 
  - Say "please" and "thank you"
  - Avoid gossip
  - Be open to feedback
  - Put yourself in others' shoes
- Good manners
  - Opens doors for others and offer your seat
  - Have table manners (elbows off tables, wait until everyone is served)
- Effective communicator
  - Listen properly
  - Be confident
  - Be direct and clear (don't be ambigious)
  - Avoid problem solving all the time, sometimes people just want to be heard.
- Effective listener
  - Maintain eye contact
  - Avoid interrupting
  - Listen without judgement
- Self control
  - Keep onself composed in situations.
  - Don't embaress yourself.
- Have interests/hobbies:
  - Read (anything!)
  - Creative (drawing, photography)
  - Physical activity (running, martial arts)
- Dress well
  - Jeans (black)
  - Flat front chinos (black)
  - Shirts (white, light pink, blue)
  - Blazers (navy, cream)
  - Suit (navy, black)
  - Footwear (leather oxfords, chelsea boots, loafers, chukka boots)
  - Coat (Peacoat or overcoat, suede jacket)
  - Watch (classic watch)
