# My Biohacking experiment

### Achieving my optimal body state

This is based on hunter gathers, reportedly had a BMI of 21-22 [1]. <br>

BMI Formula:<br>
BMI = weight / height^2<br>
weight = BMI * height^2<br>
weight = 22 * 1.8^2<br>
weight = 71.28 kg<br>
weight = 72 kg (rounded up)<br>

This means I need to get my weight down to: 72kg. I am currently approx 84kg.
However, 72kg for my body frame is a little too skinny, instead I am going to aim for 75kg.
That means I need to lose approx 9kg.

Losing weight is as simple as calories in - calories out. If i expend more calories than I consume I will lose weight.

Having healthy nutrition is by far the easiest way to lose weight. 
My strategy is going to be have meat and salad. 
Have a fixed eating window - first meal at 12 and dinner at 6.


### Physical exercise
Signed up to the gym on the 25.09.24. 
The plan is to go gym as often as I can. 
This alongside muay thai which i do 2x a week should put me on 
the path to hitting my target weight.
