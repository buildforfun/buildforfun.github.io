# My Biohacking experiment

### Achieving my optimal body state

This is based on hunter gathers, reportedly had a BMI of 21-22 [1]. <br>

BMI Formula:<br>
BMI = weight / height^2<br>
weight = BMI * height^2<br>
weight = 22 * 1.8^2<br>
weight = 71.28 kg<br>

This means I need to get my weight down to: 71kg. However, 71kg for my body frame is a little too skinny, instead I am going to aim for under 80kg first then reevaluate.
